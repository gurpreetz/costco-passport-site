Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Introduction

Costco Passport ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Costco Passport collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Location Data: We use your device's location services to help you find nearby Costco warehouses and to enable check-in functionality. Location data is processed locally on your device.
  • Visit History: Information about your Costco warehouse visits, including dates, locations, and your favorite purchases.
  • App Usage Data: Statistics about your app usage, including unlocked achievements and progress tracking.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide app functionality:

  • Display nearby Costco warehouse locations
  • Record and track your visits
  • Calculate statistics and unlock achievements
  • Provide a personalized experience within the app

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers or third parties. Your data remains on your device and is backed up through your iCloud account if you have iCloud backup enabled for the app.

Location Services

Costco Passport requires location services to function properly. You can control location permissions through your device's settings. The app will have limited functionality if location services are disabled.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

Costco Passport is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Control location permissions through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
